Best States to Move To If You're Under 40

It's no secret that young professionals are willing to relocate to make their next career move. The question that often arises: Where should I go? How do I know what's best for me? The latest census data can help us understand what states are great places to look!

At a glance, there isn't a clear correlation between age and income at the state level. This means that the median age of a state isn't the best indicator of the median salary there. This is good news for people of all ages looking to make a career jump soon!

The state that immediately jumps out to us may come as a surprise to you. With a median age of 30.5 and a median income of about $61,000, its clear there's an energetic and productive business community. Another place that surprised is Alaska. Despite its remoteness, its low median age of 33 and median income of over $71,000 make it seem appealing. Of course, you'll need to cosider other factors such as the cost of living and the kind of work availabe before making the big move.

If you're stuck somewhere like West Virginia or Arkansas, where median age is on the higher side and median income is depressed, it might be time to hit the road!
